What file formats are supported?

Dashboard Generator supports a range of file formats for effortless data integration. You can upload CSV (Comma-Separated Values) files, as well as Excel files in .xlsx and .xls formats. This compatibility ensures you can work with data from spreadsheet applications, databases, or data management systems. Our platform automatically parses and prepares your files for visualization, streamlining the process.

Can I share my dashboards?

Yes, sharing is seamless with Dashboard Generator. Export dashboards as high-quality PDFs for reports or presentations, or create secure, shareable links for online access by team members or stakeholders. Role-based access controls allow you to manage permissions, ensuring only authorized users can view or edit. This feature supports collaborative workflows, delivering insights efficiently and securely.

Is my data secure?

Security is paramount. Dashboard Generator uses AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit, adhering to industry standards. We implement rigorous access controls and conduct regular security audits to comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations. Your files and dashboards are stored securely in our cloud, with the option to delete data at any time, ensuring your information remains protected.
